# [GD] WEAPON - Great Sword

## Main character - Weapon - Great Sword

## Overview

The Great Sword is a heavy weapon class focused on devastating single hits and powerful charged attacks. It rewards patient, deliberate play with high damage per hit but slower attack speed.

---

## Main Combo

### Light Combo (4 Hits)

| Hit | Animation | Damage | Notes |
|-----|-----------|--------|-------|
| 1 | Horizontal slash right | Base | Standard opener |
| 2 | Horizontal slash left | Base | Follow-up |
| 3 | Overhead chop | Base x1.2 | Slight delay |
| 4 | Upward slash | Base x1.5 | Finisher, knockup |

**Timing**: Each hit can be delayed for timing adjustment
**Cancel Points**: After hits 1, 2, 3 into dodge or block

### Heavy Combo (4 Hits)

| Hit | Animation | Damage | Notes |
|-----|-----------|--------|-------|
| 1 | Charged overhead slam | Base x2 | Can be charged further |
| 2 | Spinning slash | Base x1.8 | Wide AoE |
| 3 | Rising slash | Base x1.5 | Launch potential |
| 4 | Diving slam | Base x3 | Ground pound, massive damage |

**Charge Mechanic**: Hold attack button to increase damage up to x1.5 multiplier
**Hyper Armor**: Hits 3-4 have super armor frames

### Jump Attack

- **Input**: Attack while airborne
- **Animation**: Plunging stab downward
- **Damage**: Base x2.5
- **Properties**:
  - Ground pound effect
  - AoE on landing
  - Can be charged while falling

### Sprint Attack

- **Input**: Attack while sprinting
- **Animation**: Running thrust into overhead slam
- **Damage**: Base x2
- **Properties**:
  - Long range gap closer
  - Hyper armor during thrust
  - Staggers most enemies

## Playstyle Notes

- **Strengths**: Highest damage per hit, excellent stagger, good range
- **Weaknesses**: Slow recovery, vulnerable during swings, stamina intensive
- **Ideal Against**: Armored enemies, bosses, single targets
- **Avoid Against**: Fast swarm enemies, quick bosses
