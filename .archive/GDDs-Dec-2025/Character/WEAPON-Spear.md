# [GD] WEAPON - Spear

## Main character - Weapon - Spear

## Overview

The Spear is a versatile polearm weapon with excellent reach and quick thrust attacks. It excels at controlling space and punishing enemies at medium range.

---

## Main Combo

### Light Combo (4 Hits)

| Hit | Animation | Damage | Notes |
|-----|-----------|--------|-------|
| 1 | Quick thrust | Base | Fast poke |
| 2 | Thrust | Base | Follow-up |
| 3 | Slash | Base x1.1 | Wider arc |
| 4 | Heavy thrust | Base x1.3 | Finisher, pierce |

**Timing**: Very fast, can rapid-fire
**Range**: Longest of all weapon types

### Heavy Combo (5 Hits)

| Hit | Animation | Damage | Notes |
|-----|-----------|--------|-------|
| 1 | Lunging thrust | Base x1.5 | Gap closer |
| 2 | Sweep | Base x1.3 | Low sweep, trips |
| 3 | Upward slash | Base x1.4 | Anti-air |
| 4 | Spinning thrust | Base x1.6 | 360 degree |
| 5 | Charged pierce | Base x2 | Full penetration |

**Charge Mechanic**: Final hit can be charged
**Movement**: Combo moves character forward

### Branch Combo

- **Input**: Light > Light > Heavy
- **Animation**: Two quick thrusts into spinning sweep
- **Damage**: Varied based on hits
- **Properties**:
  - Excellent crowd control
  - Quick transition to heavy finisher
  - Covers wide area

## Playstyle Notes

- **Strengths**: Excellent range, fast attacks, good poke game
- **Weaknesses**: Lower damage per hit, less AoE than other weapons
- **Ideal Against**: Fast enemies, maintaining distance, single targets
- **Avoid Against**: Large groups (limited AoE), hyper armor enemies
