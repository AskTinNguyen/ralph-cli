# DỤC - DESIRE Biome

## Overview

The Desire biome represents temptation, greed, and unfulfilled wants. Enemies here try to lure, trap, and corrupt the player.

---

## Biome Characteristics

### Visual Theme
- Gold and purple hues
- Luxurious but decayed
- Treasure everywhere (traps)
- Seductive imagery

### Audio Design
- Alluring whispers
- Coins jingling
- Silk rustling
- Tempting voices

### Environmental Hazards
- Treasure traps
- Illusion walls
- Addiction zones (slowing)
- Golden prisons

---

## Combat Design Philosophy

In the Desire biome, combat emphasizes:
1. **Temptation**: Risky rewards during combat
2. **Traps**: Hidden dangers everywhere
3. **Deception**: Enemies use illusions
4. **Greed Punishment**: Taking bait has consequences
