# AI - DESPAIR Biome

## Overview

The Despair biome embodies hopelessness and melancholy. Its insect-themed enemies and decaying environments create a sense of being overwhelmed and worn down over time.

---

## Biome Characteristics

### Visual Theme
- Decayed, rotting vegetation
- Insect hives and cocoons
- Muted, desaturated colors
- Constant rain or dripping

### Audio Design
- Buzzing and chittering
- Dripping water
- Mournful ambient music
- Exhausted breathing sounds

### Environmental Hazards
- Sticky webs (slow movement)
- Poison spores
- Collapsing ground
- Swarm zones

---

## Despair Biome Enemies

### Beetle
- **Type**: Tank/Bruiser
- **Behavior**: Slow, relentless advance
- **Special Ability**: Shell defense - high armor from front
- **Weakness**: Attack from behind or sides

### Dragonfly
- **Type**: Flying harasser
- **Behavior**: Fast dive attacks, hard to hit
- **Special Ability**: Hover and strafe
- **Weakness**: Vulnerable during dive recovery

### Myrmi (Ant Soldier)
- **Type**: Swarm unit
- **Behavior**: Attacks in coordinated groups
- **Special Ability**: Chemical communication - alerts others
- **Weakness**: Individually weak, separate from group

### Mantis
- **Type**: Elite assassin
- **Behavior**: Patient, strikes with lethal precision
- **Special Ability**: Camouflage, one-shot grab attack
- **Weakness**: Loud movement, can be baited

### Moth
- **Type**: Support/Debuffer
- **Behavior**: Hovers, spreads debuff powder
- **Special Ability**: Blindness dust, sleep spores
- **Weakness**: Fragile, dies quickly if caught

### Burrow
- **Type**: Ambush
- **Behavior**: Underground, emerges to attack
- **Special Ability**: Tunnel network, can appear anywhere
- **Weakness**: Vibration detection reveals position

### Bootie (Grub)
- **Type**: Creep/Fodder
- **Behavior**: Slow crawl, explodes on death
- **Special Ability**: Suicide explosion
- **Weakness**: Kill from range, avoid death burst

---

## Combat Design Philosophy

In the Despair biome, combat emphasizes:
1. **Attrition**: Constant small threats wear player down
2. **Debuffs**: Status effects are common
3. **Swarms**: Crowd control is essential
4. **Resource Management**: Healing is scarce
