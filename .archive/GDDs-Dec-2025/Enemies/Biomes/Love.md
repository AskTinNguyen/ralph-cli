# ÁI - LOVE Biome

## Overview

The Love biome represents attachment, obsession, and the pain of loss. Enemies here fight to protect or possess, creating emotionally complex encounters.

---

## Biome Characteristics

### Visual Theme
- Pink and white hues
- Soft, flowing architecture
- Heart motifs
- Fading/ghostly elements

### Audio Design
- Melancholic melodies
- Whispered voices
- Heartbeat rhythms
- Crying sounds

### Environmental Hazards
- Charm traps (confusion)
- Binding chains
- Memory fog (disorientation)
- Possessive spirits

---

## Combat Design Philosophy

In the Love biome, combat emphasizes:
1. **Protection**: Enemies protect each other
2. **Attachment**: Enemies may grab/hold player
3. **Sacrifice**: Enemies may sacrifice for allies
4. **Emotional Manipulation**: Charm and confusion effects
