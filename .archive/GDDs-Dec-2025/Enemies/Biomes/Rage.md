# NỘ - RAGE Biome

## Overview

The Rage biome embodies uncontrolled anger and destruction. Everything here is designed to test the player's ability to maintain composure under pressure.

---

## Biome Characteristics

### Visual Theme
- Orange and red flames
- Destroyed/broken structures
- Volcanic elements
- Smoke and ash

### Audio Design
- Roaring sounds
- Explosions
- Breaking/smashing
- Primal screams

### Environmental Hazards
- Erupting geysers
- Collapsing structures
- Fire waves
- Explosive barrels

---

## Combat Design Philosophy

In the Rage biome, combat emphasizes:
1. **Escalation**: Fights get harder the longer they last
2. **Destruction**: Environment changes during combat
3. **Enrage Mechanics**: Enemies power up when hurt
4. **Pressure**: Constant threat, no safe moments
