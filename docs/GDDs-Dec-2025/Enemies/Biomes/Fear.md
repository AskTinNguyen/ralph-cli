# CỤ - FEAR Biome

## Overview

The Fear biome represents one of the seven emotional realms in S2. This dark, oppressive environment is designed to evoke feelings of dread and vulnerability through its enemy design, environmental hazards, and audio-visual atmosphere.

---

## Biome Characteristics

### Visual Theme
- Dark, shadowy environments
- Limited visibility
- Unsettling organic architecture
- Eyes watching from darkness

### Audio Design
- Ambient whispers
- Sudden silence breaks
- Heartbeat-like percussion
- Distant screams and howls

### Environmental Hazards
- Darkness zones (reduced visibility)
- Fear traps (jump scares with damage)
- Paranoia mist (hallucination effects)

---

## Fear Biome Enemies

### Fear1
- **Type**: Ambush predator
- **Behavior**: Hides in shadows, strikes when player is vulnerable
- **Special Ability**: Fear gaze - causes player to slow down
- **Weakness**: Light-based attacks

### Fear3
- **Type**: Stalker
- **Behavior**: Follows player at distance, attacks from behind
- **Special Ability**: Invisibility when not moving
- **Weakness**: AoE attacks reveal location

### Fear6
- **Type**: Nightmare manifestation
- **Behavior**: Appears from nowhere, teleports
- **Special Ability**: Creates shadow clones
- **Weakness**: Must destroy all clones to damage main body

---

## Combat Design Philosophy

In the Fear biome, combat emphasizes:
1. **Awareness**: Players must constantly check surroundings
2. **Reaction**: Enemies attack unpredictably
3. **Light Management**: Light sources are tactical resources
4. **Sound Cues**: Audio telegraphs are crucial for survival
