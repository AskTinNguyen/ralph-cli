# Ố - HATRED Biome

## Overview

The Hatred biome represents pure aggression and hostility. Enemies here are relentlessly aggressive, and the environment itself feels antagonistic toward the player.

---

## Biome Characteristics

### Visual Theme
- Red and black color palette
- Sharp, angular architecture
- Blood-like rivers and pools
- Burning elements

### Audio Design
- Aggressive percussion
- Growling ambient sounds
- Metal clashing
- Rage-filled screams

### Environmental Hazards
- Fire traps
- Spike pits
- Crushing walls
- Lava flows

---

## Combat Design Philosophy

In the Hatred biome, combat emphasizes:
1. **Aggression**: Enemies attack relentlessly
2. **Damage Trading**: High damage on both sides
3. **No Retreat**: Enemies pursue without rest
4. **Berserker Mechanics**: Low HP enemies become more dangerous
