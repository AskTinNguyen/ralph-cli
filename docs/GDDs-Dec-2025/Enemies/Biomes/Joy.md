# HỶ - JOY Biome

## Overview

The Joy biome represents happiness twisted into mania and chaos. Enemies here are unpredictable and chaotic, with bright colors masking danger.

---

## Biome Characteristics

### Visual Theme
- Bright, clashing colors
- Carnival/circus elements
- Distorted happy imagery
- Glittering but dangerous

### Audio Design
- Manic laughter
- Carnival music (off-key)
- Jingling bells
- Sudden loud noises

### Environmental Hazards
- Trap doors
- Spinning platforms
- Confetti explosions (damage)
- Fun house mirrors (disorientation)

---

## Combat Design Philosophy

In the Joy biome, combat emphasizes:
1. **Unpredictability**: Random attack patterns
2. **Chaos**: Multiple simultaneous threats
3. **Misdirection**: Fake tells and feints
4. **Mania**: Enemies speed up over time
